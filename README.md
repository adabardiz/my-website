# my-website
My personal website which is also a project for the NASA Stardance challenge.

My website consists of three sections: About, Projects & Minigame

- The 'about' section basically summarizes who I am in two sentences -- yes, there's not much to tell I'm a pretty basic person.

- The 'projects' section isn't that detailed, giving three examples of my recent projects/works. The section contains two links: my GitHub accounts link and my LinkedIn accounts link. Click them if you want to connect or if you want to learn more about who I am & what I do!

- Lastly at the bottom of a page there's a minigame where you can give my dog (pictured on the top of the page) some pets by pressing a button. Press 10 times and there's an easter egg!
